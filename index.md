I'm a queer, Boricua, molecular biologist and science writer.

In my thesis research, I use **cryo-electron microscopy and electrophysiology** to study the structure and function of channelrhodopsins.

I'm also a **strategic and evidence-based** science communicator. My work aims to increase trust in scientists by fostering community wellbeing and citizen engagement with science.
